About this device

Tested and working, V7 1500VA 2U rackmount UPS (EU version), over USB.

V7 UPS1RM2U1500-1E UPS 1500VA Rack Mount 2U supported by blazer_usb

Shutdown sequence tested via

:; /usr/local/ups/sbin/upsmon -c fsd

shuts down as expected.

Reported at:

  • https://github.com/networkupstools/nut/issues/716
